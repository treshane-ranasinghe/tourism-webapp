Weligama

A Surfer’s Paradise

Weligama, located on the southern coast of Sri Lanka, is a stunning
coastal town known for its golden beaches and crystal-clear waters.
The town offers a serene atmosphere perfect for relaxation, with
breathtaking sunsets and gentle waves ideal for surfing. Beyond
the beach, Weligama invites visitors to explore its rich local culture,
delicious seafood, and warm, welcoming community. Whether you're
seeking adventure or tranquility, Weligama promises an unforgettable
experience amidst natural beauty and vibrant heritage.
